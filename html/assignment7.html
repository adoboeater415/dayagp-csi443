Website Review

Responsive Navigation and Structure - overall the navigation is pretty responsive and applies appropriate breakpoints. The menus responsiveness is spot-on and not at all confusing for the user. I think it follows "best practices" as it's navigation scheme is pretty effective in terms of navigating throughout the website's multiple pages.

Responsive Design - just like the navigation, the design is pretty effective. The hierarchy of the items listed on the pages from desktop view all the way to mobile are ordered accordingly. It effectively used the flexsliders to showcase it's newest products right in front of the users without compromising the rest of it's contents.

Content - do they look fresh? YES! As I have mentioned above, MSI has effectively placed it's newest products where it matters. In terms of responsiveness of the content, all content seem to be fluid enough that they are very responsive for any given breakpoints.
